About us

RotaCare is a web app to assist NHS nursing staff with their rota management.


The application will allow the nursing staff users to request specific shift times and days, request holidays, swap shifts between staff (in the same department where they have the same job title) and visualize their timetable allocated for the whole month at once.


Every Charge nurse user on the department will have all the privileges as the nursing staff, and additionally, they will have the ability to accept requests for a specific shift or holiday day sent by the rest of the staff. Charge nurses will also be able to view the staff name, job title and shift time for each specific day of the month.
